HOW TO PLAY

🎯

Drag pieces onto the board

Grab a piece from the tray below the board and drop it on the 8×8 grid. Pieces can't overlap or hang off the edge.

✨

Clear lines for points

Fill an entire row or column and it clears. Clear multiple lines in one move for a combo bonus.

📦

Earn stash slots

Every line you clear unlocks a new hold slot below the tray. Drag a piece into the stash to save it for later.

💡

Use hints

Stuck? Tap the 💡 button on the board for a quick highlight of where your next piece fits. 15-second cooldown.

🎮

Modes for every mood

Classic for chill, Sprint for speed, Hardcore for pain, Custom for chaos, Daily for fairness, Multiplayer for friends, Fruit Drop and Line for variety. Have fun.
